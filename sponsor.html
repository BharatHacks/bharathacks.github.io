Why Sponsor BharatHacks

BharatHacks is a hackathon for solving India-specific problems. Programmers, designers, engineers, and entrepreneurs will come together to build products to make lives beter.

Hire the top tech talent

We've collaborated with Facebook Developer Circles Delhi-NCR Chapter and community partners such as DelhiStartups and Startup Nexus, along with college representatives to ensure the best hackers at the event. Delhi-NCR's top developers, designers, and managers will be present, and you company will get a direct opportunity to hire them, straight out of BharatHacks.

Get developers to build for your platform

Our technology partners like IBM Global Entrepreneur, Aadhaar Bridge, and Neuron are helping developers build using their platforms and SDKs. By sponsoring BharatHacks, you can showcase your platforms and technologies to Delhi-NCR's top developers, and help them build on your platform. Get your developer platform in front of the right eyes.

Get your brand out there in front of hundreds

With an incredibly diverse community of programmers, investors, and innovators, BharatHacks is the perfect place to showcase your brand. Your company will be in front of hundreds of participants and a panel consisting of the whos-who of the Indian startup ecosystem.
